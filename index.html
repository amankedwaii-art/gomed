Uniform TBM Case Definition (2010)

Marais et al. (2010) — developed to standardize TBM research and improve diagnostic reproducibility globally.

Category Max Score
Clinical criteria (duration, systemic, neuro)6

Classification (without imaging):
  • Definite TBM: Positive CSF AFB smear, culture, or PCR
  • Probable TBM: Score ≥10, no alternative diagnosis
  • Possible TBM: Score 6–9, no alternative diagnosis
  • Unlikely TBM: Score <6
Classification (with imaging):
  • Definite TBM: Positive CSF AFB smear, culture, or PCR
  • Probable TBM: Score ≥12, no alternative diagnosis
  • Possible TBM: Score 6–11, no alternative diagnosis
  • Unlikely TBM: Score <6
